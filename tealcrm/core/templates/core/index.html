🏠 Welcome to Teal CRM

Simplifying Lead & Client Management!

🔹 Teal CRM is a full-stack Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system built using Django & Tailwind CSS, designed to help businesses manage leads, clients, teams, and workflows efficiently.

Key Features:

  • ✅ User Authentication – Secure signup, login, and admin access
  • ✅ Dashboard Tracking – View recent 5 leads & clients
  • ✅ Lead & Client Management – Full CRUD operations
  • ✅ Lead-to-Client Conversion – Retains comments & file uploads
  • ✅ Plan-Based Access – Control max leads & clients based on plans
  • ✅ Data Export – Download leads/clients as CSV or Excel
  • ✅ Team Switching – Seamlessly switch between teams
